# DenGrow Demo Script

**Duration:** 1-2 minutes
**Audience:** Stacks ecosystem, investors, developers

---

## Opening (10 sec)

> "DenGrow turns daily habits into real-world impact. Let me show you how."

---

## 1. Connect Wallet (15 sec)

1. Open the app at `localhost:3000` (or deployed URL)
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the navbar
3. Approve the Hiro Wallet connection
4. Show: Network selector (Testnet/Mainnet)

> "Connect with Hiro Wallet. Everything runs on Stacks, secured by Bitcoin."

---

## 2. Mint a Plant (20 sec)

1. On the home page, click **"Mint Your Plant"**
2. Approve the transaction in wallet
3. Wait for confirmation
4. Navigate to **"My Plants"**

> "Minting creates your unique plant NFT. Each one has random traits — pot style, background, companion, and plant species."

---

## 3. Show Plant Detail (20 sec)

1. Click on the newly minted plant card
2. Point out:
   - **Stage**: Seed (starting point)
   - **Progress**: 0/7 growth points
   - **Traits**: Unique combination
   - **Dynamic SVG**: Visual representation

> "Your plant starts as a Seed. The goal is to grow it into a Tree through consistent daily care."

---

## 4. Water the Plant (20 sec)

1. Click the **"Water"** button
2. Approve transaction
3. Show the progress bar update: 0 → 1/7
4. Explain cooldown (testnet = instant, mainnet = 24 hours)

> "Each water adds one growth point. After 7 waters, your plant evolves to the next stage. Seed → Sprout → Plant → Bloom → Tree."

---

## 5. Impact Dashboard (20 sec)

1. Navigate to **"/impact"**
2. Show the dashboard:
   - Trees in Pool
   - Total Graduated
   - Total Redeemed
   - Progress bar

> "When plants reach Tree stage, they graduate to the Impact Pool. We batch-redeem these for real tree planting, all recorded on-chain with verification proofs."

---

## Closing (15 sec)

> "DenGrow gamifies environmental impact with blockchain transparency. Every tree grown, every redemption — verifiable on Stacks forever."

> "Try it on testnet today. Links in the description."

---

## Key Talking Points

If asked questions:

- **"Why Stacks?"** — Bitcoin security, Clarity smart contracts, transparent state
- **"How do redemptions work?"** — Admin records batches with proof hashes and URLs
- **"What makes it unique?"** — Daily engagement loop + real-world impact + on-chain proof
- **"Future plans?"** — Leaderboards, social sharing, seasonal events, sponsored batches
